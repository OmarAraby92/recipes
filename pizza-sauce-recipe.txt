Ingredients:
-onions (chopped)
-salt
-pepper
-tomatoes (diced or chopped)
-olive oil 
-ground chilli
-garlic (minced)

Directions:
1- heat the oil in a sauce pan over medium heat
2- saute the chopped onions
3- add in the chopped tomatoes and garlic
4- add the rest of the ingredients to taste
5- simmer for a couple of minutes
6- empty the sauce pan in a food processor and give it a stir
